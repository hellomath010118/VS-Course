Get every department + official credits & descriptions in one go (no extension needed)
  1. Log in to ASC and open Academic → All About Courses → Running Courses; pick the year & semester so the department list shows.
  2. Press F12 → Console tab. (If pasting is blocked, type allow pasting and press Enter first.)
  3. Copy the grabber script , paste it into the console, press Enter.
  4. Wait a minute or two — it downloads asc-courses-….json (runs entirely in your browser, on your own session). Drop that file above.

Be kind to ASC: a grab is ~1300 small requests, so one friend grabs per semester and shares the .json — it holds only public course pages (no login tokens, nothing personal), which is exactly why it's shareable.
